1
Alison Wonderland
Run
Welcome to the contradictory world of Alison Wonderland; an Australian artist who has written an album during her time in Los Angeles, a public personality who privately obsesses over her music with near-maniacal intensity; an electronic producer who is equally proud of her pop songs and her festival bangers. (via The Windish Agency)
Listen on Spotify
2
Aurora
Running With The Wolves
Norwegian singer-songwriter Aurora quickly became one of Norway’s most talked about artists with the release of her EP Running With The Wolves. Her electric stage presence will have you hooked the moment she walks on stage.
Listen on Spotify
3
Bring Me The Horizon
Throne
It’s a story of reinvention. It was the first step in a process that has culminated in the breathtaking That’s The Spirit. Ambitious, grand, emotional and complex, it’s a record that demands the band be seen in a new light. (via Alternative Press)
Listen on Spotify
4
Bryson Tiller
Just Another Interlude
By the time the singer-rapper released his debut mixtape, T R A P S O U L, that initial offering had been streamed more than 25 million times. "I'm waiting for an artist like him," Sango explains. (via Rolling Stone)
Listen on Spotify
5
Danny Seth
I Arise
Enter Danny Seth, one of the hottest musical exports from the United Kingdom in 2015. The 21-year-old London native has caused a serious impact on the American rap hemisphere with a stark myriad of contrasting sounds and a distinct flow. (via Hypetrak)
Listen on Spotify
6
Gallant
Gold
The rising alt-R&B singer-songwriter began recording songs in middle school to parse his teen emotions. "It started to be a cathartic thing," he says, "the same way that you would write something in your diary." (via Billboard)
Listen on Spotify
7
Halsey
New Americana
On Badlands, Halsey's larger-than-life vision combines the synthy darkness of Lorde, the neon-pop chutzpah of Miley Cyrus and the flickering film noir of Lana Del Rey. But all that escapist fantasy is fed by gritty reality. (via Billboard)
Listen on Spotify
8
Jack Garratt
The Love You're Given
Hailing from London, the 24-year-old multi-instrumentalist has quickly gained fans across the globe with a pair of EPs and another pair of singles over the last year. Using only his own musical and production gifts, he crafts a sound that sits somewhere between alternative indie music and the current R&B vogue, something that’s as likely to draw comparisons to Disclosure as Ed Sheeran. (via COS)
Listen on Spotify
9
Lido
Faded
Hailing from the humble country of Norway, Lido is a relatively name new in the EDM world, only rising to popularity in the last year. With a style difficult to categorize in any formal segment of the EDM scene, Lido has managed to entice listeners with his genre-bending production and keen sense of rhythm, which is likely why he was placed between an R&B singer and a singer-songwriter. (via BREHM)
Listen on Spotify
10
Melanie Martinez
Sippy Cup
Melanie Martinez is an unforgettable 20-year-old artist from Long Island, New York – a stunning and provocative singer, songwriter and visual presence. Her debut Atlantic album, Cry Baby, was released on August 14, 2015 and debuted as the “Hot Shot Debut” at #6 on the Billboard Top 200. (via Soundcloud)
Listen on Spotify
11
Mura Masa
Firefly
Named after Japanese sword-smith Muramasa Sengo, the Guernsey-born musician comes armed with deft beats, exotic instrumentation and heartbreaking lyrics. Like his namesake, he says he wants the music "to cut like a knife". Only 19 years old, he has already created a strong body of original material, incorporating Asian soundscapes (Shakuhachi flutes, thumb pianos) into his glitchy electronica.
Listen on Spotify
12
Nothing but Thieves
Itch
In an era where tracks are produced by committee and honed by focus groups, Nothing But Thieves are a rare beast indeed – a band with the talent, integrity and vision to create heartbreakingly beautiful music entirely on their own terms. (via MTV)
Listen on Spotify
13
Pretty Vicious
Cave Song
Pretty Vicious, straight outta Merthyr Tydfil in Wales, are one of those outfits that come along every once in a while and supposedly signal a return to “proper” music, a four-square rock’n’roll band talking about teen anger and frustration using the tried and tested format of guitar/bass/drums, topped off with phlegmy, belligerent vocals. (via Guardian)
Listen on Spotify
14
Raury
Revolution
Singer, songwriter, guitarist, rapper, producer: Raury is a musical prodigy, who wrote his first song - Oh Little Fishy - at the age of three. “It's not just his songwriting and sonic versatility that sets Raury apart from the rest, he also has enough charisma to propel him into the superstar bracket.” - Tom Cotton, Head Of Music, Amazing Radio. (via BBC)
Listen on Spotify
15
Years and Years
Desire
Rooted in 90s R&B – Years & Years cite TLC and and Aaliyah as influences. “Infectious and uplifting beats, I love the energy of this band. The dancey vibe is combined with soulful vocals giving their tracks a lovely rich vibe.” Kate Blakemore, Music Supervisor, Lime Pictures. (via BBC)
Listen on Spotify
